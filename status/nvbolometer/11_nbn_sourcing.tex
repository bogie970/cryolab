\label{sec:nbn-sourcing}

$T_c \geq 15.7$\,K. This section documents the sourcing analysis and recommended
procurement path.

\subsection{UT Arlington Shimadzu NRC: Feasibility Assessment}

The Shimadzu Institute NRC (Arlington, TX; \url{https://shimadzuinstitute.org/centers/nanotechnology-research-center/})
has confirmed on-site:
\begin{itemize}
  \item AJA ATC ORION Series UHV sputtering system
  \item Kurt J.\ Lesker Lab~18 sputtering system
  \item Home-built sputter system
\end{itemize}
Reactive DC magnetron sputtering of Nb in N$_2$/Ar ambient on sapphire achieves
$T_c = 16.58$\,K at 200\,nm film thickness and $\sim$150$^\circ$C substrate temperature
(source: \url{https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/10697750/}).

\textbf{What is NOT yet confirmed:}
\begin{enumerate}
  \item Whether an Nb sputtering target is installed or available.
  \item Whether N$_2$ reactive gas is plumbed into any system.
  \item User fee rate (per-hour or per-run for sputtering).
\end{enumerate}

\textbf{Recommended action:} Email \texttt{nanofab@uta.edu} with the following queries:
(1)~Is an Nb target (or NbN target) available? (2)~Is N$_2$ reactive gas plumbed into the
AJA ORION or KJL system? (3)~Per-hour fee for sputtering? (4)~Prior NbN experience on-site?

Nb sputtering targets are commercially available ($\sim$\$200--500 from Heeger Materials:
\url{https://heegermaterials.com/sputtering-targets/463-niobium-nitride-sputtering-target.html}).

\subsection{Recommended Path}

\begin{enumerate}
  \item \textbf{Primary: UT Arlington NRC in-house deposition.} Jacob is at UT Arlington;
    no shipping or MOU delays; direct process control. Estimated turnaround 4--8 weeks
    after NRC confirms N$_2$ gas and Nb target availability.
    % internal: hardware/nbn_film_sourcing.md Sec.~5 (primary recommendation rationale)

  \item \textbf{Secondary: AJA International contract deposition.} If UT NRC cannot
    support NbN. Jacob supplies sapphire substrate; AJA deposits NbN to spec.
    Contact: \texttt{topgun@ajaint.com}; lead time $\sim$3--6 weeks.

  \item \textbf{Tertiary: 2D Semiconductors.} Expedient proof-of-concept only.
    Standard product has $T_c \approx 12$\,K (below 15\,K requirement); custom sapphire
    substrate must be requested. Cost: \$590/sample (2026-03-19).
    \textbf{Not recommended for D0.}
\end{enumerate}
